About

Nutrio makes calorie tracking simple, fast, and stress-free, so you can focus on your goals instead of numbers. Log meals in seconds and get clear insights into what you’re eating every day.

Built for real life, Nutrio helps you understand your habits with smart summaries and easy-to-read progress tracking. Whether you’re losing weight, gaining muscle, or maintaining balance, Nutrio adapts to your lifestyle.
